# Design System: Sammamish Circle
**Project ID:** 13342369628992888286

## 1. Visual Theme & Atmosphere
Cinematic, dark-themed, and premium. The aesthetic is deep and moody, utilizing high-contrast accents (Marigold) against deep structural bases (Teal and Obsidian). It feels functional, airy yet structured, and state-of-the-art.

## 2. Color Palette & Roles
* **Deep Structural Teal (#244747):** Primary background for depth and cinematic feel.
* **Obsidian Black (#000000):** Structural sections and contrasting borders.
* **High-Contrast Marigold (#FFC300):** Primary CTAs, active states, and interactive hotspots.
* **Pristine White (#FFFFFF):** Primary text for maximum contrast on dark surfaces.

## 3. Typography Rules
Clean sans-serif (Plus Jakarta Sans). 
* **Headers:** Large, bold, tightly kerned (700 weight, -0.02em letter-spacing).
* **Labels:** Uppercase, heavily spaced out microcopy.

## 4. Component Stylings
* **Buttons:** Taut rectangular shapes with Marigold backgrounds and Black text.
* **Cards/Containers:** Subtly rounded corners (8px), deep Teal or Black backgrounds, using whitespace and thin white borders (1px) for definition instead of heavy shadows.
* **Backdrops:** Cinematic glassmorphism with subtle blurs and glow effects.

## 5. Layout Principles
Functional-first grid alignment with deliberate whitespace. Alternating sections of Deep Teal and Obsidian Black. 
```
Design System Notes for Stitch Generation:
- Use #244747 for backgrounds
- Use #FFC300 for Marigold highlights
- Typography: Plus Jakarta Sans

```
