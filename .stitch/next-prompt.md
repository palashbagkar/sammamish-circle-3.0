A cinematic, dark-themed homepage for the Sammamish Circle community hub. It must feel premium, deep, and highly functional. 

**DESIGN SYSTEM (REQUIRED):**
*   **Deep Structural Teal (#244747)**: Primary base background for most sections, offering a deep, cinematic depth.
*   **Obsidian Black (#000000)**: Used for alternating structural sections, deep contrasting borders, and foundational structure to create a moody, premium feel.
*   **Pristine White (#FFFFFF)**: Primary text color across all dark surfaces (Teal and Black) to ensure maximum contrast and legibility. Also used sparingly for high-contrast light panels.
*   **High-Contrast Marigold (#FFC300)**: Primary call-to-action buttons, active states, and interactive highlight points (e.g., geospatial hotspots).
*   **Typography**: Clean sans-serif. Large, bold, tightly kerned hero headings (e.g., `font-weight: 700`, `letter-spacing: -0.02em`). Uppercase, heavily spaced out microcopy for labels.
*   **Component Stylings**: Deep Teal or Black flat cards utilizing deliberate whitespace instead of muddy drop-shadows. Thin white borders define edges. Taut rectangular buttons with Marigold backgrounds and Black text.

**Page Structure:**
1. **Header Navigation**: Deep Teal top bar with crisp, white uppercase links and a Marigold CTA button.
2. **Cinematic Hero**: Full-width immersive image overlaid with a deep gradient (Teal to Black). Large white display heading and a prominent Marigold primary action button.
3. **Featured Community Resources**: A curated grid section using alternating Black/Teal backgrounds. Cards are flat with thin white borders, showcasing high-quality images and crisp white text.
4. **Newsletter Section**: Deep Black structural base with highly legible white inputs and Marigold submission button.
5. **Footer**: Clean tabular layout of white links on a Deep Teal base.
